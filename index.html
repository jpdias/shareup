What is ShARE World Seminar?

The ShARE World Seminar happens once in a year and gathers representatives from all ShARE teams. It has speakers that will aboard topics that help the audience to open the horizons. This speech are open to not only to share members but also to all community. It is the most important event for ShARE members and the best opportunity for members from different countries to physically meet.

Previously ShARE World Seminar:

  • Mumbai - "Building a collective conciousness" - 2013
  • Shangai- "Asia, the Innovation Factory" - 2012
  • Bangalore- "Emerging Globally" - 2011
  • Shanghai- "Better Business, Better Life" - 2010

What we are?

ShARE is an international students think-tank. A multicultural, non-profit organization for students that strives to better understand the complexities of local socio-economic issues through presentations, conferences and projects. ShARE's first team was created in Shanghai in November 2002. Today, more than 600 members in Shanghai, Beijing, Hangzhou, Nanjing, Seoul, New Delhi, Kolkata, Indore, Ahmedabad, Roorkee, Lucknow, Kharagpur, Bangalore, Mumbai, Lahore, Jakarta, Kathmandu, Kampala, Dhaka, Sao Paulo, Istanbul, Lausanne, Porto, Warsaw, Moscow, Oslo and Paris gather regularly to discuss economic and social issues in Asia, Europe, Africa and Latin America.

With the emergence of a global knowledge and information society, ShARE’s vision is to generate, share and use knowledge to contribute to regions’ human, social and economic development.
Find out more in our local website and apply or in our international website.

António Portela

5 March - 2:30PM

FEP :: Salão Nobre

“Barriers to export products to the Asian Market”

António Portela is currently the CEO of Bial, one of the biggest pharmaceutical companies in Portugal which its products are to be found in pharmacies in more than 40 countries in 4 continents: Europe, America, Africa and Asia. In the past he also worked at Roche Pharmaceuticals as product manager. Due to his position, António Portela has a clear vision about the challenges and differences between markets of different continents and methods to transpose those adversities especially concerning health products.

Luis Reis

5 March - 5PM

FEP :: Salão Nobre

“Strategy an Innovation on Europe’s Businesses”

Luis Reis is the Chief Corporate Centre Officer of Sonae, the largest Portuguese retail company with two major partnerships in the shopping centers and telecommunications sectors. Having responsibilities in organization processes and corporate changes, Luis Reis deals with the internal and external communications, investor relations, public affairs and information systems implementation. Adding to this, he is also President of APED (Retail Portuguese Association) and currently lectures Economics and Management at the University of Porto.

António Vidigal

4 March -

FEUP :: Auditório B032

Antonio Vidigal has professional experience on Innovation, Telecommunications, Computer Science and Power Systems. He is presently General Director at EDP Group, with responsibility over Innovation. He is also CEO of EDP Inovação and Executive Board Member of EDP Ventures. Both companies are deeply involved in the selection and fostering of new technologies in the Clean Tech domain, for usage within EDP. Additionally, António Vidigal has had the opportunity to have key roles in several Technology start-ups (Edinfor which became at the time the largest IT company in Portugal, Optimus, OniWay). He has published more than a 100 articles on technology and innovation.

Eduardo de Oliveira Fernandes

4 March - 2PM

FEUP :: Auditório B032

“Energy Efficienci: an insight from China”

Eduardo de Oliveira Fernandes is a jubilated teacher from the Faculty of Engineering of Porto, who dedicated his life and work to finding innovative solutions in the fields of Energy Efficiency, Health and Environmental Concerns. Having had a diverse professional background, Professor Eduardo Oliveira Fernandes has been the Secretary of State for the Environment for the Portuguese Government, in charge of defining Energy Efficiency Policies for the country, President of the Portuguese Solar Energy Society, a Professor and Researcher in 3 different Continents, among many other positions. He has also been a Consultant for several Organizations and International Projects such as INDEX, HOPE and PRESCO.
