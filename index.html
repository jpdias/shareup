António Portela

António Portela is currently the CEO of Bial, one of the biggest pharmaceutical companies in Portugal which its products are to be found in pharmacies in more than 40 countries in 4 continents: Europe, America, Africa and Asia. In the past he also worked at Roche Pharmaceuticals as product manager. Due to his position, António Portela has a clear vision about the challenges and differences between markets of different continents and methods to transpose those adversities especially concerning health products.

Luis Reis

Luis Reis is the Chief Corporate Centre Officer of Sonae, the largest Portuguese retail company with two major partnerships in the shopping centers and telecommunications sectors. Having responsibilities in organization processes and corporate changes, Luis Reis deals with the internal and external communications, investor relations, public affairs and information systems implementation. Adding to this, he is also President of APED (Retail Portuguese Association) and currently lectures Economics and Management at the University of Porto.
